   Jim Sullivan likes music. He plays many instruments. He plays the piano, clarinet, 
saxophone, trumpet, guitar, and bagpipes. The bagpipes are his favorite instrument to 
play. Not very many people play the bagpipes. Jim plays the bagpipes for celebrations. 
He also plays the bagpipes in parades. The audience listens to the bagpipes. They clap 
for Jim. They enjoy the music of the bagpipes. Jim also teaches people how to play the 
bagpipes. He gives lessons to children and adults. He teaches them the history of the 
bagpipes. He teaches them how to play music with the bagpipes. Jim is a good teacher.
